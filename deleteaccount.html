Delete Your Account

How to request deletion of your GiftCircles account and data

If you'd like to delete your GiftCircles account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the GiftCircles app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete account.
  3. Confirm your request. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  4. If you can't access the app, contact us with the subject Account Deletion Request.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your user account and authentication information (email, username)
  • All wish list items and associated metadata
  • Locally stored preferences

What Data Is Retained

  • Transaction records (subscriptions and payments) required by Google Play or law for up to 5 years.

Need Help?

For any questions about account deletion, contact us.
